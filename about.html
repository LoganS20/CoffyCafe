Our Story

The Coffy Cafe was first opened in 2017 by Saya after a trip to South America, where they witnessed the impact of regional coffee production on the local community.
Inspired by the diligent workers of South America, Saya reached out to their long time friend and business consultant Lewis. Together they sourced local coffee beans from Northland and opened a small pop up shop in Grey Lynn.

The unique and fragrant taste of the South American brew technique immediately captivated locals and travellers alike. As Coffy Cafe started to take off, Saya and Lewis decided to bring aboard two coffee extraordinaires, Renee and Logan, to bring new ideas for brewing into the process.
The cafe has since gone from strength to strength, winning multiple Auckland Business Innovation and Hospitality awards.


The Team Today

Our humble team has remained consistent throughout the years, with Saya's passion of discovering new flavours and techniques, Logan and Renee's drive to provide top quality hospitality to our loyal customers, and Lewis's careful craftmanship in putting together our meals and drinks.
