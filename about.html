Our Story

The Coffy Cafe was first opened in 2017 by Saya after a trip to South America where they could see the impact of reagional coffee production on the local community.
Inspired, Saya reached out to their long time friend and business consultant Lewis and together they sourced local coffee beans from Northland and opened a small pop up shop in Grey Lynn.

Although there were mixed feelings initially for the 'unripe' tasting coffee, the business soon grew and took on two more staff, Renee and Logan, for the Front of house.
The cafe has since gone from strength to strength winning multiple Auckland Business Inovaiton and Hospitality awards.


The Team Today

The small team remains the same consisting of Saya as head inovator, Logan and Renee at front of house and Lewis now works in the kitchen
